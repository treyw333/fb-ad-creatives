Facebook Changed the Rules

In 2023, Meta rolled out Andromeda, a new retrieval engine that scores ad quality in real time. The old playbook of outbidding competitors no longer works — creative quality is now the primary driver of delivery and cost efficiency.

Before

Highest Bid Wins

Advertisers competed primarily on budget and bid strategy. Mediocre creatives could still reach audiences if you spent enough money.

After

Creative Quality Wins

Andromeda evaluates creative quality signals — engagement, relevance, and conversion likelihood — before deciding who sees your ad. Better creative = lower costs.

How to Spot Winners and Losers

Winner Signals

Low cost-per-result
CTR above 1.5%
Strong hook rate (video)
Improving over 3+ days

Loser Signals

Rising cost-per-result
CTR below 0.8%
High frequency (audience fatigue)
Declining after day 2

Simple A/B Testing That Works

          

            

              Recommended
              
CBO — Campaign Budget Optimization

              
Set one budget at the campaign level and let Facebook distribute spend to the best-performing ad sets automatically. Less micromanagement, faster learning.

            

            

              
ABO — Ad Set Budget Optimization

              
Set individual budgets per ad set for tighter control. Useful when testing very different audiences, but requires more hands-on management.

            

          

          

            
Start with CBO, 3–5 ad variations per ad set, and $20–50/day minimum. Give each test 3–5 days before making decisions.
